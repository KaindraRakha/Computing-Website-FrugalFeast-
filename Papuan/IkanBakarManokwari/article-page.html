Ikan Bakar Manokwari

March 15, 2025

Ikan Bakar Manokwari is a simple yet bold dish consisting of freshly grilled fish topped with a raw chili-lime sambal, unlike other Indonesian grilled fish, the sambal is not cooked. It's spicy, fresh, and served with warm rice. This version keeps it affordable and traditional.

Information

Total Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 4 Servings

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Prepare the Fish: Clean and scale the fish. Make diagonal slits on both sides. Rub with crushed garlic and salt. Let sit for 10 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    Make the Sambal: Finely chop the chilies and shallots. Mix with salt, lime juice, and boiled water. Set aside.

  3. Step 3

    Grill the Fish: Heat grill or pan over medium heat. Brush fish with oil. Grill for 5â€“7 minutes per side until cooked through and skin is crispy.

  4. Step 4

    Serve: Place grilled fish on a plate. Spoon sambal over the fish. Serve with steamed rice and cucumber slices on the side.
