Directions

  1. Step 1

    Prepare Bananas: Peel and cut bananas in half lengthwise or leave whole (based on size). Lightly press them flat with the side of a knife or spoon.

  2. Step 2

    Make the Batter: In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, rice flour, cornstarch, salt, sugar, and baking powder. Add cold water gradually while stirring until a thick but smooth batter forms (like pancake batter).

  3. Step 3

    Heat the Oil: Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat. The oil should be enough to submerge the fritters.

  4. Step 4

    Fry the Bananas: Dip banana pieces fully into the batter. Gently drop into hot oil and fry until golden and crispy (~5â€“7 minutes). For "rambut kriuk" (crispy flour web), drizzle thin batter into the oil before adding bananas, then wrap the crispy strands around them.

  5. Step 5

    Drain and Serve: Remove and drain on paper towels. Serve hot as-is or with powdered sugar, chocolate drizzle, or cheese (optional toppings not included in budget).
