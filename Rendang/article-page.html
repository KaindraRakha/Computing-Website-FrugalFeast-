Rendang is a rich and tender coconut beef stew which originates from West Sumatra, Indonesia. Known for its deep, aromatic flavor and spicy kick, it's traditionally slow-cooked until the meat is coated in a thick, caramelized spice paste. This version is simplified for home cooks seeking bold flavor without a big cost.
