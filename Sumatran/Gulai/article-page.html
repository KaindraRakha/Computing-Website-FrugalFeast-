Gulai is a deeply spiced curry dish popular across Indonesia. With a coconut milk base and bold spice paste, Gulai Ayam (chicken gulai) delivers rich, aromatic flavors. This affordable version uses basic ingredients while maintaining authentic taste and warmth.

Servings: 5 Servings
